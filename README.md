## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
- Launch Retentive
- Click "Sign Up" to create a new account
- Enter your email and password
- Start your learning journey!

### 2. Create Your First Topic
- Click "New Topic" in the sidebar
- Enter a topic name (e.g., "Spanish Vocabulary")
- Add items - one per line (e.g., "Hola - Hello")
- Choose your learning mode:
  - **Ultra-Cram**: Intensive daily reviews
  - **Cram**: Accelerated learning
  - **Steady**: Balanced pace
  - **Extended**: Relaxed intervals

### 3. Start Learning
- Go to the Topics page
- Click "View Items" on your topic
- Review items as they become due
- Rate your recall: Again, Hard, Good, or Easy
- Watch your progress grow!

### 4. Track Your Progress
- Check your level and points in the header
- View achievements in the Gamification dashboard
- Monitor your streak - keep it going!
- Analyze your performance in the Stats page

## Mastery System

After 5 successful reviews, choose what happens to mastered items:

- **Archive**: Remove from active reviews permanently
- **Maintenance**: Continue reviewing at 2x intervals
- **Repeat**: Reset and start from the beginning

## Tips for Success

1. **Review daily** - Even 5 minutes helps maintain your streak
2. **Be honest** - Rate your recall accurately for optimal intervals
3. **Use the right mode** - Match the urgency of your learning needs
4. **Stay consistent** - Regular short sessions beat irregular long ones
5. **Archive completed items** - Keep your review queue manageable
